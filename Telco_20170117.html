Date¶

Tuesday, 17th January, 16:30 Brussels Time

Hangout Link: https://plus.google.com/hangouts/_/j72qwlvegiojjpt3a36pfhow5ua

Agenda¶

  • Welcome and agree Agenda

  • PR #527 clarify if item (group, field, or attribute) is required or optional (examples)

  • PR #529 update copyright dates to 2017

Minutes¶

Present: TSR, RO, MK, PJ

Pull requests accepted and closed.

Ticket opened for clarifying status of “anonymous” groups (without a specific required name) in the documentation. That is currently rendered as “(name)” without the qoutes, which is not obvious.

AOB: Ray suggests we are present at the RDA meeting and remote-data-pre-meeting in Barcelona in April. He will provide links via mail. “LInks to RDA” set on the agenda for next time.
