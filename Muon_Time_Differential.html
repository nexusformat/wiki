Muon Time Differential¶

The Instrument Definition for the Muon instruments has evolved from that proposed in 2001 to the more robust definition outlined in the linked discussion document.

For completeness both are discussed in this document; however, the NIAC are asked to vote on and ratify the ‘Muon Instrument Definition: Version 2 - pulsedTD’

Aspects of the Muon Definition either introduce new NeXus Classes or extend existing definitions. These are discussed separately in the linked document, and the NIAC are asked to separately consider and ratify these elements that are key to the Muon definition.
